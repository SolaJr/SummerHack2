Mission

This summer, many students lost their internships. Please help us as we host a 2-weeks, virtual hackathon for students to build, network, and learn. We are providing students with summer projects, networking opportunities, and speaker series from industry professionals.

Program Structure

Feature section

Students form teams (2-5) via Jumpstart based on roles and ideas as well as host Guests Speakers online!

Early June

Teams can work on challenges with Zoom integration while attending various speaker series for teams and individuals to learn from.

Late June

Teams will submit 3 minute demo videos, and we will pick the top 10 teams to pitch to the public.

Awards

In addition to the award of the hackathon project you build this summer, you will also receive awards such as gift cards and sponsor awards for creating amazing projects or creatively using a sponsor tools or APIs. We also provide awards for our top 3 projects.

Guest Speakers

We will be having industry professionals speaking on topics throughout the duration of the hackathon. We will also have student speakers giving advice on how to land a job or get an internship after interning at top Tech companies like Facebook.
